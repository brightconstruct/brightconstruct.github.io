About Forms Translator ‚Äì Unified Responses

Forms Translator is a powerful Google Workspace Add-on that allows you to translate your Google Forms into multiple languages while keeping all responses organized in one unified spreadsheet. Perfect for international surveys, multilingual events, and global data collection.

Key Features

  • Multi-Language Translation: Translate your forms into any language supported by Google Translate.
  • Unified Response Collection: All responses from translated forms automatically consolidate into one spreadsheet.
  • Auto-Sync Updates: Changes to the original form automatically propagate to all translated versions.
  • Preserve Formatting: Maintains form structure, images, sections, and question types across translations.
  • Language Mapping: Automatically tracks which language each response came from.
  • Easy Management: Simple sidebar interface for managing translations and viewing unified data.

How It Works

  1. Install the add-on from the Google Workspace Marketplace.
  2. Open your Google Form and launch Forms Translator.
  3. Select target languages and generate translated copies.
  4. Share translated forms with your international audience.
  5. View unified responses - all languages combined in one spreadsheet.
  6. Update once, sync everywhere - changes propagate to all translations.

Use Cases

  • International Surveys: Collect feedback from global audiences in their native languages.
  • Multilingual Events: Registration forms for conferences, workshops, or webinars with international attendees.
  • Educational Institutions: Collect data from students and parents who speak different languages.
  • Customer Feedback: Gather product reviews and satisfaction surveys across different regions.
  • HR & Onboarding: Multilingual employee surveys and onboarding forms for diverse workforces.
  • Research Studies: Academic or market research requiring responses in multiple languages.

    Google Forms (Read & Edit Access)

    Purpose:

    • Read the structure of the active form (questions, options, sections, images)
    • Create translated copies of the form in other languages
    • Maintain a relationship between the original form and its translated versions

    Specific Scopes:

    • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/forms.currentonly (Access the currently open form)
    • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/forms (Create and update translated forms created by the Add-on)

    Limited Use:
    The Add-on only accesses forms that you explicitly open, translate, or generate through the Add-on. It does not scan or access unrelated forms in your Google Drive.

    Google Sheets (Read & Edit Access)

    Purpose:
    The Add-on requires access to Google Sheets in order to:

    • Read responses from translated forms
    • Store internal language mappings and configuration metadata
    • Generate unified response views (e.g., Unified Responses and Unified Form View)

    Specific Scope:

    • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/spreadsheets

    Limited Use:
    Only the response spreadsheet linked to your form is accessed. No other spreadsheets in your Google Drive are read or modified.

    Google Apps Script UI (User Interface)

    Purpose:
    This scope allows the Add-on to display user interface elements such as sidebars, dialogs, progress indicators, and help messages inside Google Forms.
